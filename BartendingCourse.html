Bartending Certification

$595 USD

What You’ll Learn – All aspects of industry-level bartending skills taught by professional bartenders/state license teachers.

  • All the modern bar tools & equipment
  • Free-pouring & Jigger proficiency
  • Modern-day cocktail-making that includes all the classics.
  • Spirits & Liqueur knowledge / wine & beer service
  • Opening/Closing Procedures
  • Customer Service/Register Procedures
  • Efficiency skills behind the bar

Baruch Bartending School® provides students with industry-level bartending skills that will allow them to work in bars worldwide, as well as home mixologists looking for a fun, hands-0n learning experience in the art of mixology.

Prerequisite – Must be 18 or over. No additional bartending experience required.
Tuition: $595 (For bundle prices and payment plans, please visit the school).

Schedules: Full-day (1-week) accelerated bartending classes start every Monday; evening (2-weeks) start every other Monday; Saturday & Sunday bartending classes start every month. Scroll down to view starting dates.

  • 5 Accelerated Days: Monday-Friday. 1-week from 9 am to 5 pm + unlimited practice
  • 8 Evening Classes: Monday-Thursday 2-weeks from 6 pm to 9.30 pm + unlimited practice.
  • Four Saturdays: Four consecutive Saturdays from 9.30 am to 4.30 pm + unlimited practice.
  • Four Sundays: Four consecutive Sundays from 9.30 am to 4.30 pm + unlimited practice.

Students can take as long as is needed to complete and pass the exam. We don’t care if you take 40-hours or 40-days; there will never be an additional fee! Scroll down to register online, or drop by the school and register in-person. We have an open door policy. Text 212.768.8460 if you have any questions.
